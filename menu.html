Espresso

Espresso is made by forcing nearly boiling water through finely-ground coffee beans, which results in a concentrated, syrup-like coffee drink.

Latte/Iced Latte

A latte is an espresso with steamed milk and a dollop of milk foam on top.

Black Coffee

Black coffee is arguably the most common type of coffee drink out there.

Mocha

Mochas are espresso-based drinks made with chocolate and whipped cream.

Americano

The Americano is another popular type of coffee drink.

Cappuccino

Cappuccinos are a classic Italian type of coffee drink made of espresso, steamed milk, and thick foam.

Flat White

Flat whites are a type of coffee drink that's made with espresso and steamed milk.

Cafe au Lait

Cafe au lait is a coffee beverage that is made with dark roast coffee and steamed milk.

Macchiato

The word "macchiato" means spotted, so a macchiato espresso is an espresso with a splash of milk.

Cold Brew

Cold brew coffee is created by steeping coarsely ground coffee in cool or room temperature water for an extended period.

Frappe

A coffee frappe is a delicious, iced beverage that has been blended or beaten until it forms a foamy texture.

Red Eye

Red Eye Coffee is a favorite among coffee drinkers because of its unique and rich flavor.
